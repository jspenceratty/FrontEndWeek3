Below is a list of some of my most favorite things:

# Item Comments
1 Dad Dad is the best. He takes the best care of me. I am so glad I chose him so many years ago.
2 Sleep This is a very close second to Dad. I like to sleep most of the day. Dad has a 'supervisory chair' set up for me next to him in the office. He put a kitty warming pad on it so I stay nice and warm.
3 Licksies Dad calls these treats. They are a puree of some sort--they come from a metallic-looking pouch. I typically get to enjoy my licksies after I have gone out in the garage to look at the stuff out there. Sometimes I even encounter things like mice and snakes out there. There's a whole world to explore out there.
4 Cat nip Some times Dad gives me a "hit of the nip". I do so like cat nip. It makes me feel all relaxed and crazy at the same time. I don't get it very often, but I sure do relish it.
5 Toys I have little balls that are very fluffy. They are stored in bags with cat nip. I like to throw these around and run after and bat at them. Sometimes, I even rip them apart with my teeth. I even get pieces of the balls stuck in my mouth. Dad sometimes has to help me getting that fuzziness off.
